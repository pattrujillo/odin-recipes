Kabobs

These grilled kabobs made with steak and chicken stay moist and flavorful. The meat and vegetables are marinated in a teriyaki sauce, then skewered and grilled until tender and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Sauces: Teriyaki sauce and honey
  • Seasonings: Garlic powder and ground ginger
  • Meat: Chicken breasts and a pound of beef sirloin
  • Vegetables: Bell peppers, sweet onions, and fresh mushrooms

Steps

  1. Make the marinade
  2. Marinate the meat and vegetables
  3. Thread the ingredients onto skewers
  4. Grill the kabobs over medium-high heat.
