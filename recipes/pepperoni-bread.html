Pepperoni bread

Pepperoni bread is like pepperoni pizza rolled into a loaf of bread. This recipe is so easy and always turns out great. Everyone loves it because who can resist the delicious taste of pizza?

Ingredients

  • 1lbs loaf frozen bread dough, thawed
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 4 ounces sliced pepperoni
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Place thawed dough into a bowl; cover and let rise in a warm place until doubled in volume, about 1 hour.
  2. Pre-heat the oven to 375 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Roll dough into a rectangle, then brush with beaten egg and arrange pepperoni over top. Sprinkle mozzarella, Parmesan, and Italian seasoning over pepperoni. Roll dough over fillings like a jelly roll and pinch the seam to seal. Place dough, seam-side down, onto the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 40min.
  5. Use a bread knife to cut loaf into slices of your desired size.
